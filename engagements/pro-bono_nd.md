Rather than working against my tendencies, I learned to work with it and design my personal system around it.
# What I offer
I help professionals who fall through the cracks to become the designers of their personal operating systems.

The program consists of 1-to-1 virtual sessions where we study the patterns that shape your lives through: 
- Observe: Increase awareness of facts, assumptions, and believes. 
- Explore: Understand the factors influencing your progress towards your goal. 
- Experiment: Test strategies in practice and learn from the results.

The program is most suitable for those with a clear goal but struggle to make or sustain meaningful progress.

The goal is not to force yourself into a situationally-specific approaches, but to develop a system that can adapt to different situations within your current realities and constraints.

- Smoothening task transitions
