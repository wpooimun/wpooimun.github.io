The link to make an appointment is at the end of the document.

# What I offer
I help people become the designers of their personal operating systems that can adapt to different situations within their current realities and constraints.

The program consists of 1-to-1 virtual sessions where we study the patterns that shape your lives through:
- **Observe**: Increase awareness of facts, assumptions, and believes.
- **Explore**: Understand the factors influencing your progress towards your goal.
- **Experiment**: Test strategies in practice and learn from the results.

The process is often reflective, experiential, and adaptive, rather than prescriptive, theoretical, and situationally-specific.

# Example Topics
- Clarifying priorities and decision-making
- Navigating career transitions in technical fields
- Improving planning and execution
- Overcoming procrastination
- Smoothening task transitions
- Regulating attention and energy
- Shaping sustainable ways of working
- Designing routines and habits

## Support Philosophy
I repeated observed that when an individual's personal system align with their lifestyles, responsibilities, strengths, and constraints, many things that once felt difficult often become easier to sustain.

Rather than viewing setbacks as failures, I view them as information that can help us make adjustments and discover approaches that fit you. My role is not to prescribe the "right" way to live or work, but to support you in developing solutions that are both effective and sustainable.

# Availability and Fees
Individuals referred through selected professionals may be eligible for a different fee arrangement intended to improve accessibility of support.

Availability is subject to capacity. If all available places are occupied, you may choose to:
- **Option 1:** Join the waiting list until a place for such fee arrangement becomes available.
- **Option 2:** Proceed immediately at the prevailing standard rate.

Waiting time varies over time and availability is not guaranteed.

# Important Boundaries
This program is developmental in nature. It is not therapy, treatment, diagnosis, or other licensed professional services. Information shared and discussed is also not intended to replace any legal, financial, or medical advice.

Some individuals may benefit from developmental support alongside other professional services, such as therapy or consultation. Where appropriate, you may also be encouraged to seek support from other professional services.

# Confidentiality
Information shared during sessions will be treated as confidential, except where disclosure may be required by law or where there are serious concerns relating to safety.

Information will not be shared with the Professional Referrer unless requested by you and supported by your written consent.
